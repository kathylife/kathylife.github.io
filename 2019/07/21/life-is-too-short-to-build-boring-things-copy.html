Life is Too Short to Build Boring Things

Jul 21, 2019

I have updated the About section of my website a little bit. It now includes some of the questions that I constantly ask myself and reflect upon:

Are you bored by what you do on a daily basis? Can the boring tasks be handled by robots instead? Can YOU contribute to the design of new robots? What are your ethical guidelines?

What are you uniquely qualified to do? Are you doing what you are uniquely qualified to do? If not, why not?

Are you proud of what you do? Will you still be proud of what you do 10 years from now? What about 50 years from now?

What are your dreams? Are there any steps you can start taking today to get closer to those dreams? If you don’t start taking the steps, will you regret it 10 years later? What about 50 years later?
